what:

An unofficial Twilio international outbound rates API.

endpoint:

http://twilio-rates.appspot.com/calculate/{number}/{duration}
number: the outbound international number
duration: the call duration in minutes

example:

http://twilio-rates.appspot.com/calculate/18778894546/3

{"status": "OK", "country": "United States", "rate": "0.030",
"total_rate": "0.09", "duration": "3"}
